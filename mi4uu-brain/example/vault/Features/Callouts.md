# Callouts

Obsidian-flavor `> [!type]` blocks. Five built-in types, each with its
own colour token from the theme.

> [!note] Note
> A note pulls the reader's attention to a side remark without
> interrupting flow.

> [!tip] Tip
> Tips celebrate a clever shortcut or a non-obvious idea worth
> remembering.

> [!warning] Warning
> Warnings flag something the reader needs to be cautious about.

> [!danger] Danger
> Danger blocks mark destructive operations or critical mistakes to
> avoid.

> [!info] Info
> Generic info blocks for background context.

## Nested

> [!tip] Pro tip
> Callouts can nest paragraphs, lists and even other markdown:
>
> - Item one
> - Item two with `inline code`
> - Item three with a [[Wikilinks|wikilink]]
